### Template A: Chikankari Suppliers (Lucknow)

```
Subject: Wholesale Inquiry - Children's Chikankari Collection | Style Saplings Boutique

Namaskar [Supplier Name] ji,

I am writing from Style Saplings Boutique, a premium children's ethnic wear brand
specializing in traditional Indian craftsmanship for kids aged 2-5 years.

We are developing our Chikankari collection and are looking for a reliable manufacturing
partner in Lucknow. Here is what we need:

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:
- Children's kurta sets (kurta + pyjama) in sizes 2Y, 3Y, 4Y, 5Y
- Children's A-line kurta/tops for girls in the same size range
- Base fabric: Pure cotton (mulmul/voile preferred) - must be soft, breathable, and
  safe for children's sensitive skin
- Embroidery: Traditional Chikankari hand embroidery (tepchi, bakhiya, murri, phanda
  stitches preferred)
- Colors: White-on-white, pastels (soft pink, mint, lavender, cream)
- No synthetic fabrics, no harsh chemicals or dyes

INITIAL ORDER:
- Sampling: 2-3 pieces per design per size (for quality assessment)
- First production order: 25-50 pieces per design (across sizes)
- Future orders: Scaling to 100-200 pieces per design

QUESTIONS:
1. Do you currently produce children's Chikankari garments?
2. Can you work with our designs/patterns, or do you offer existing children's designs?
3. What is your minimum order quantity?
4. What is the price range per piece for kids kurta sets with hand Chikankari?
5. What is your typical production lead time?
6. Can you provide fabric and embroidery samples before we place an order?
7. Do you offer Aari and hand-embroidery, or machine embroidery?

We are building a long-term relationship with our suppliers and value quality,
authenticity, and fair artisan practices.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
Style Saplings Boutique
[Phone / WhatsApp]
[Email]
[Website]
```

## NOTES

- **Verification:** All contact details were sourced from public web listings (IndiaMART, company websites, JustDial, ExportersIndia) as of May 2026. Verify phone numbers and emails before sharing sensitive business information.
- **IndiaMART tip:** Many suppliers are listed on IndiaMART. Creating a free buyer account gives you access to direct contact details and the ability to send RFQs (Request for Quotation) to multiple suppliers simultaneously.
- **Fabric safety:** For children's clothing, always request OEKO-TEX or equivalent safety certification, and confirm azo-free dyes are used.
- **Sample first:** Always order samples before committing to bulk production. Assess fabric softness, embroidery quality, colorfastness (wash test), and sizing accuracy.
- **Payment terms:** Most Indian wholesale suppliers expect advance payment (50-100%) for first orders. Negotiate better terms (30-50% advance, balance on delivery) once a relationship is established.
